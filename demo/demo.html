FAQ

  • What's jQuery Thought Bubble?
    • Thanks for asking. It's a small plugin for attaching a thought bubble to any element.
  • What is customizable?
    • The text, color, font, and animation speed can all be modified with plugin attributes.
  • What about positioning?
    • That gets a bit tricky. Because the container ".bubble-container" is absoluteley positioned, you may need to adjust the width/height of the container to get the desired affect However, even though the container is absolutely positioned, it is responsive to window resizing.
